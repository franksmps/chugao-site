Who We Are

Zhongshan Chugao Electronic Technology Co., Ltd. is a high-tech enterprise specializing in the R&D, production, and sales of LED switching power supplies. We are committed to providing efficient, stable, and energy-saving power solutions for global customers.

With years of industry experience and strong technical capabilities, Chugao has established a comprehensive product system covering adapters, indoor power supplies, waterproof power supplies, and rainproof power supplies. Our products are widely used in commercial lighting, industrial control, and outdoor applications.

Adhering to the philosophy of "Quality First, Customer Priority", we strictly control product quality to provide the best service to our partners.
