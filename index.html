About CHUGAO

Zhongshan Chugao Electronic Technology Co., Ltd. We make LED switching power supplies in a 6,000m2 factory in Guzhen, Zhongshan. 38 people on the floor, 8 in sales.

Four product lines: adapters, indoor drivers, IP67 waterproof, IP65 rainproof. Sold to 42 countries. Most orders go to lighting distributors and sign makers in the EU, US, and Middle East.

Choose the right power supply faster

For a precise quote, start from the application, then confirm voltage, wattage, IP rating, quantity, and destination market.

LED strip / modules

Adapter or indoor driver

Best for indoor signs, shelves, cabinet lighting, and short LED strip runs.

  • Confirm DC 12V / 24V / 36V / 48V
  • Add 20-30% power margin
  • Ask for plug type and label artwork
Outdoor projects

IP67 waterproof line

Best for exposed strips, fountains, facade lighting, and wet installation areas.

  • Confirm cable length and connector
  • Confirm salt-spray or high-humidity use
  • Ask for CE / RoHS PDF by model
Signage / billboard

IP65 rainproof line

Best for semi-outdoor signage cabinets where airflow and higher wattage matter.

  • Confirm 12V / 24V output
  • Confirm cabinet ventilation
  • Confirm carton weight and pallet need
OEM / distributor

Custom label or housing

Best when you need your logo, fixed SKU list, market files, or packaging control.

  • MOQ starts from 500 pcs
  • Send target label and spec sheet
  • Lead time usually 25-30 days
